When I started with Computers I first got my beloved Commodore 64 where I made the first experience with Games programming and also Games cracking - Games were too expensive and easy to crack - so we removed the copy protection and added our own little intros which were mainly some sprite animations with some raster colors or some stolen sounds or music. I will not mention my cracker name or our cracker group, however long time ago. After the C64 times I took all available money and bought an Amiga 1000 - and the first two games I remember were DOC (Defender of the Crown) and POC (Ports of Call).

Ports of Call is one of the favorite games of all time and much later I found out that the game was developed by Rolf Dieter Klein, a genius of the time who even had his own Computer TV Show on the German Television.

Anyhow, since then I have still the idea to come up with a remake of Ports of Call, but this time with real vessel data, real shipyards, real ports and all the things I am dealing today.
